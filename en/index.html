Matteo Ingoglia

We offer everything for the production of pallets and for companies in the industrial woodworking sector. We are distributors of wooden pallet production lines with automation and sawmill machinery, board cutting systems with automation made in Italy. We also have all the raw material available for the production of pallets.

We are a second-generation company with more than 35 years of experience in the wood industry. Our values are reliability, speed, transparency, responsibility, and innovation.

We collaborate with the best Italian producers in the wood industry and export the best technological solutions throughout Europe. Our customers include major companies in the wood sector, especially from Italy, Germany, Poland, and Switzerland.

Matteo Ingoglia is a European distributor of sawmills and production lines for wooden pallets made in Italy.
