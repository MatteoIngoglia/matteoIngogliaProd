Machines and Production Lines for Wooden Pallets

We offer machines and pallet lines from OMEV Erba, a company that has been operating in the sector for over 60 years. Omev produces machines and lines for the production of standard and customized pallets, cases, and reels.

We have a wide range, from small batch lines to fully automated, high-capacity, and flexible pallet production lines with up to 600 units per hour (EPAL type).

The systems are completely customizable with the addition of different machinery, allowing every production need to be satisfied. Thanks to these flexible solutions, a finished product of the highest quality is guaranteed.
