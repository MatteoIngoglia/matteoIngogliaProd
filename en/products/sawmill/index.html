We offer sawmill machines from CML Engineering

The knowledge that goes into the technology puts CML ENGINEERING Srl in a world-leading position in the manufacture of wood cutting machines and systems. Its products are characterised by low operating costs, high productivity, precision, and durability. The production of automatic circular saws is highly flexible and includes more than 30 different models.
