Wood Dryers

We offer the container wood dryers from the Italian manufacturer Incoplan.

Incoplan’s drying systems for the treatment and evaporation of wood are 100% made in Italy, with high performance and durability, efficient and sustainable.

We offer a wide range of dryer models:

  • From 8 to 300 cubic meters
  • Hot water supply 90°C, water 119°C, steam, diathermic oil, electricity, heat pump, gas burner.
  • Possibility of installation inside or outside.
  • Automatic process management system.

Applications:

  • Pallet (ISPM 15)
  • Firewood
  • Strips
  • Exotic wood
  • Hard woods
  • Conifers/resinous
