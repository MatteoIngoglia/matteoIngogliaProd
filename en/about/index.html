About us

We are a distributor of all types of machines for automatic pallet production, 100% made in Italy.

We are a second-generation family business, working every day to guarantee the best products and services in the industry. Our work is based on our core values: reliability, speed, transparency, responsibility, and innovation.

We collaborate with the best Italian manufacturers in the woodworking machinery industry and export the best technological solutions across Europe. Our customers include major companies in the wood industry in Italy, Germany, Poland, and Switzerland.

Our Partners

We collaborate with the hidden champions of the Italian wood industry, offering a wide range of innovative machines to make your production more efficient.
